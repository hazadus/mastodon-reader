💬 New article: how Go's runtime collects profiles

CPU, heap, block, mutex, goroutine. Five profiles, one pprof file, and every sample is just a call stack.

What changes is how the stack gets recorded: CPU streams it through a ring buffer, heap/block/mutex fold it into a bucket table in place, goroutine snapshots them all on demand.
