💬 Why is RAM so expensive now? Why don’t companies just manufacture more RAM to meet market demand?

RAM is made with an extreme ultraviolet lithography machine. One company located in the Netherlands, ASML, has a global monopoly on these machines. There are only dozens in the entire world. Critically important to the shortage, they will not sell to Chinese manufacturers. Many companies are working on their own, but there is a very long lead time. You cannot quickly spin up a new assembly overnight.

The move to high-bandwidth memory (smaller than 10 nanometer) that companies like Apple tout in their MacBook marketing, and is the preferred memory used for AI processing is even more advanced and demanding of the manufacturer, furthering the shortage. 👨🏻‍💻
