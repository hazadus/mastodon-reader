**🕒 13:03 👤 @dabeaz@mastodon.social**
💬 I think might greatest achievement on a Raft implementation was my effort to perform a TLA+-like verification on top of the actual implementation code. 

There's a Figure 8 in the Raft paper that shows a particularly nasty edge case that must be protected against.   I had this notion that if I intentionally left off the guard for that bug, could I write a "test" that would surface the bug under the assumption that I did *NOT* know the bug existed in the first place.

[Открыть пост](https://mastodon.social/@dabeaz/116222000911915432)
