💬 Came across this [#GoLang](https://dusty.ninja/tags/golang) Dev mailing list post about [#AI](https://dusty.ninja/tags/ai) tool usage, and it seems a bit tighter(?) than the [#Linux](https://dusty.ninja/tags/linux) Kernel.
